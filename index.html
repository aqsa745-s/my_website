Welcome to the Department of Mathematics

Our department focuses on providing quality education and research opportunities in mathematics. We aim to develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills among our students. Our faculty is committed to guiding students in both theoretical and applied mathematics. We offer courses ranging from basic algebra and calculus to advanced mathematical modeling and analysis. Students are encouraged to participate in seminars, workshops, and collaborative research projects. Our department strives to create an environment that fosters innovation, curiosity, and academic excellence. Graduates of our program are well-prepared for careers in education, industry, and further research in mathematics.

Programs Offered

  • Bachelor of Science in Mathematics (B.S)
  • Ph.D. in Mathematics
  • Applied Mathematics and Computational Modeling
  • Statistics and Data Analysis
  • Mathematics Education and Research
