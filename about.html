About the Department

The Department of Mathmatics was established in 2000 with a vision to provide excellence in education.

Vision

  • To be a leading department in innovative research and education.
  • To develop skilled graduates for the global IT industry.

Mission

  1. Provide high-quality teaching and learning environment.
  2. Encourage research and development.
  3. Promote collaboration with industry and academia.
Message from the Chairperson: "Our students are our priority. We focus on both knowledge and character development."
